2022 Federal Solar Tax Credit

Install your project before 2023 to qualify for the 2022 tax credit.

The Federal Solar Tax Credit will credit up to 30% of your total solar project amount. On average, a homeowner can get back $12,300. You could do a lot with that kind of money. Here are a few ideas:

  • Invest in yourself by applying that credit to your solar loan.
  • Buy iced out diamond grills to let people know you're the realest.
  • Buy 4 Shetland ponies to make your wildest dreams come true.

Whether it's grills, ponies, or your solar loan, we want you to benefit from the 2022 tax credit, and the only way you can do that is by installing by the end of the year.

As with all good things your tax credit could take time. Install now to begin that process so you can claim your money as soon as possible.

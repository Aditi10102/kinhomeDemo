2022 Federal Solar Tax
Credit






Living in Texas is great!

you get great barbecue, beautiful plains, and a vibrant history. You also have a choice when it comes to your electricity provider. We want to make sure that you're getting the most out of your solar investment and getting paid for the excess solar power your panels produce.

Our goal is to always keep you informed of the best solar buyback options available to you in Texas. Some of the best features available from providers today include:

  • Unlimited rollover of your solar credits
  • No cap on bill credits
  • Fixed energy rate buy back
  • No annual credit sweep
  • Credits applied to EVERYTHING on your statement (incl. delivery
    charges, base fee, and taxes)

We recommend visiting powertochoose.org to view available plans in your area. You’ll want to make sure the provider you choose offers a "solar buyback program", or "net metering” program.

We recommend Rhythm Energy to get the best bang for your buck with your solar buyback plan.

Please let us know if you have questions - we are happy to help, and to provide additional resources to help empower you as a solar homeowner.
